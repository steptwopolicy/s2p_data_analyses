Techical Notes

  1. Beds = The number of Residential Beds the facility is approved to operate.
  2. Beds Available = Available Residential Beds reported by facility. This figure should include any licensed beds that are not operational.
  3. Census (aka “Occupied Beds”) = (Beds) - (Beds Available).
  4. Data exclude pediatric ventilator beds and dialysis station beds.
